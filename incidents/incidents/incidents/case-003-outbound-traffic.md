# Case 003: Suspicious Outbound Traffic

## Summary
Detected unusual outbound traffic from a Windows VM during testing.

## Symptoms
- Unexpected connections to non-standard ports  
- High outbound packet rate  

## Investigation
- Packet capture with Wireshark  
- Identified destination IPs (simulated safe lab)  
- Cross-checked with Event Viewer for processes initiating traffic  

## Root Cause
Simulated suspicious activity generated by test scripts  

## Resolution
- Logged incident with mitigation steps  
- Verified normal traffic restored after remediation  

## Outcome
Demonstrated ability to monitor, identify, and document abnormal traffic patterns.
